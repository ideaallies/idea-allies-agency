# Technology Research & Exploration

This directory contains exploratory research documents that inform strategic technology decisions for Idea Allies.

## Purpose

Research documents gather information and explore options **before** making decisions. They are inputs that lead to decision records in `../decisions/`.

## Research Statuses

- **In Progress:** Actively researching
- **Complete:** Research finished, ready to inform a decision
- **Archived:** Moved to `../04-archive/` after decision is made and implemented
